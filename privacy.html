Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how R Pizza and More (located in Tajpur, Samastipur) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application and online ordering system.

This application was developed by an independent developer on behalf of R Pizza and More to facilitate local food delivery and pickup orders.

1. Information We Collect

To provide you with food delivery services, we collect the following necessary information when you place an order:

  • Name: So we know who the order is for.
  • Phone Number: To contact you regarding your order status, delivery, or if we have questions about your items.
  • Delivery Address: Used strictly to deliver your food to your location.
  • Order Details: The food items you selected and any special instructions.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively for order fulfillment. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third-party marketing or advertising agencies. We use your data to:

  • Process and prepare your food order.
  • Send your order details to the restaurant via secure messaging (Telegram).
  • Deliver the food to your provided address.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your order details are securely transmitted to the restaurant staff to fulfill your order. Your ongoing cart items are temporarily saved locally on your device so you don't lose your order if you close the app. We do not maintain a permanent online database of user accounts or passwords.

4. Third-Party Services

Our app utilizes third-party infrastructure (such as Vercel for hosting and Telegram API for order notifications) strictly to operate the ordering system securely. These services are bound by their own security policies and do not access your data for their own marketing.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact the restaurant directly at our physical location in Agarwal Complex 0005, Tajpur, Samastipur.
